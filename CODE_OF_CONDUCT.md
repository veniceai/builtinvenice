# Code of Conduct

Built with Venice is a community showcase. The goal is to make it a welcoming, useful resource for everyone building in the Venice AI ecosystem.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and submissions.
- Submit work honestly — describe what your project actually does, not what it markets itself as.
- Credit collaborators where appropriate.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language toward other contributors or maintainers.
- Spam submissions, duplicate listings, or attempts to game the showcase.
- Submitting projects you do not have the right to represent, or misrepresenting affiliation with Venice AI, Inc.
- Marketing copy, scam tokens, or projects that exist primarily to drive traffic without offering real ecosystem value.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may close issues, decline submissions, edit content, or block contributors who violate these guidelines. For serious or repeated violations, contact the maintainers listed in [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md).

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), kept short to match the scope of this project.
